# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 19, 2025_

## 1. Overview

Worksite Leads respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## 2. Information We Collect

- Contact details (e.g., name, email, phone)
- Business information
- Payment and billing information (processed through secure third-party processors)
- Usage data (e.g., website interactions, if/when analytics are enabled)

## 3. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

- Deliver and manage services
- Communicate with clients
- Improve performance and user experience
- Process invoicing and payments

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data except:

- With trusted partners (e.g., GoHighLevel, Netlify) as required to deliver services
- When legally required (e.g., by government or regulatory bodies)

## 5. Cookies & Analytics

- No third-party analytics are active by default.
- Netlify's built-in analytics may be used anonymously.
- If Google Analytics is enabled, users will be notified.

## 6. Your Rights

- You may request to view, update, or delete your data at any time by emailing [legal@worksiteleads.com](mailto:legal@worksiteleads.com).
- EU/UK users may have GDPR rights.
- U.S. users may have local data protections depending on their state.

## 7. Security

- Data is encrypted during transmission and stored on secure platforms.
- Access to backend systems is limited to verified personnel.

## 8. Contact

For questions or concerns, email: [legal@worksiteleads.com](mailto:legal@worksiteleads.com)
